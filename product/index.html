Permafrost is a design studio consisting of four Norwegian industrial designers: Andreas Murray, Eivind Halseth, Oskar Johansen and Tore Vinje Brustad. They all graduated from the Oslo School of Architecture and have made a name for themselves with their excellent work.

The Permafrost collection is a selection of iconic design objects conceived, developed and produced by the Norwegian designers at Permafrost. All the toys in their collection have been created for different international design exhibitions and have been displayed all over the world from Milan to New York.
